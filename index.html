NWS Damage Assessment Toolkit

—

Post-event field surveys conducted by NWS meteorologists. Includes confirmed EF ratings, path length, width, injuries, and fatalities. Survey data typically available 24–72 hours after an event.

Iowa Environmental Mesonet — LSR

—

Near-realtime Local Storm Reports issued by NWS forecast offices. Compiled from trained spotters, emergency managers, and law enforcement. Generally available within hours of an event.
